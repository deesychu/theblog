Project Pink

The Quest for The Perfect Pink Lip Balm.

What is Project Pink?

Project Pink began the moment I twisted up my Sunnies Face Lip Glaze in Coquette and realized—I’d hit hit pan. Tragic. My favorite pink lip balm, gone just like that. From its sheer, sweet pink color to the satisfyingly cool metal applicator, there was no lip balm that compared. But instead of mourning, I’m making it my mission to find my new holy grail pink lip balm.
